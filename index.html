A Legacy of Excellence in Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering

Established in 2006, our department has grown into a nationally recognized, NBA-accredited center for engineering. We are committed to nurturing technical pioneers through AICTE-approved B.Tech, M.Tech, and PhD programs, addressing the critical infrastructural and industrial needs of the nation.

Our vision is to be a beacon of excellence, producing quality engineers and conducting cutting-edge research that makes a tangible impact.

Our Mission in Action

Educate & Empower

To provide a strong foundation in mechanical engineering, while instilling human values, self-confidence, and independent thinking in every student.

Innovate & Research

To conduct research that addresses critical global challenges, from sustainable energy management to the development of alternative resources and smart systems.

Serve Society

To produce high-quality engineering professionals equipped with the latest technologies to serve heavy engineering, manufacturing, and the nation at large.
